About Our Company

Founded in 2019 by a team of customer support veterans and AI researchers, KnowledgeBase was born from a simple observation: businesses were spending millions on support infrastructure while their customers struggled to find answers. We set out to change that by combining advanced artificial intelligence with an intuitive knowledge base platform. What started as a small startup with a mission has grown into a trusted solution serving thousands of companies worldwide, from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our founders' deep experience in both customer support operations and machine learning gave us the unique insight to build something truly transformative.

Today, our mission remains unchanged: to empower businesses with intelligent, AI-ready knowledge base solutions that delight customers and empower support teams. We believe that every company, regardless of size, deserves access to world-class support infrastructure.
